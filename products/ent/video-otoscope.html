Video Otoscope System

High-definition video otoscope for detailed ear canal and tympanic membrane examination with real-time image capture and documentation capabilities.

Category Diagnostic Equipment
Application Otology

Request Quote Download Brochure

Product Overview

The Video Otoscope System represents the latest advancement in ear examination technology. Featuring a high-resolution camera integrated into an ergonomic otoscope design, this system provides exceptional visualization of the ear canal and tympanic membrane for accurate diagnosis.

The system connects seamlessly to monitors, computers, or mobile devices, allowing real-time viewing, image capture, and video recording. This makes it ideal for patient education, documentation, telemedicine consultations, and training purposes.

Key Features

  • High-definition camera with adjustable LED illumination
  • Real-time image and video capture capability
  • Multiple connectivity options (USB, WiFi, HDMI)
  • Ergonomic design for comfortable extended use
  • Compatible with disposable specula for infection control
  • Built-in image processing for enhanced visualization
  • Patient database integration ready
  • Portable and lightweight design

Clinical Applications

  • Otitis media diagnosis and monitoring
  • Cerumen impaction assessment
  • Foreign body identification
  • Tympanic membrane perforation evaluation
  • Post-surgical follow-up examinations
  • Hearing aid fitting assessments
