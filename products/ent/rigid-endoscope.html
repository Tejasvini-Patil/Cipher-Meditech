Product Overview

Our Rigid Endoscope Set provides exceptional optical clarity for diagnostic and surgical procedures in the nasal cavity, paranasal sinuses, and larynx. Manufactured with premium rod lens technology, these endoscopes deliver bright, sharp images even in challenging anatomical areas.

The complete set includes multiple viewing angles to accommodate various clinical needs, from routine examinations to complex surgical procedures. Each endoscope features a durable stainless steel construction with an autoclavable design for reliable sterilization.

Key Features

  • Premium rod lens optical system for superior image quality
  • Multiple viewing angles: 0°, 30°, 45°, and 70°
  • Autoclavable stainless steel construction
  • Integrated fiber optic light transmission
  • Available in 2.7mm and 4.0mm diameters
  • Scratch-resistant optical surfaces
  • Compatible with standard light sources and cameras
  • Ergonomic eyepiece design

Clinical Applications

  • Diagnostic nasal endoscopy
  • Functional endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS)
  • Laryngoscopy and voice assessment
  • Adenoid examination
  • Nasopharyngeal assessment
  • Post-operative follow-up examinations
