Description

The NuVent™ system for balloon sinus surgery

The NuVent™ EM balloon sinus dilation system features rigid balloon sinus seekers pre-calibrated for surgical navigation with the StealthStation™ ENT navigation system during balloon sinus surgery.

Using the NuVent™ system, you can locate and move tissue, bone, or cartilage around sinus drainage pathways. After confirming placement with a StealthStation™ navigation system, inflate the NuVent™ sinus balloon to expand the sinus outflow tract.1,2

Features

How does it work?

  1. Use the NuVent™ system seeker and StealthStation™ ENT or StealthStation FlexENT™ navigation systems to locate the drainage pathway and follow it into the sinus.1,2
  2. Inflate the seeker's balloon to move tissue and bone blocking the sinus drainage path.

Medtronic sinus treatment technologies

The NuVent™ system for balloon sinus ostia dilation may be used when performing balloon sinus surgery with surgical navigation. Our wide range of sinus-focused products can help you treat a broad spectrum of sinus disease and sinus patients.

Plug-and-play with Medtronic ENT navigation

Pre-calibrated for the StealthStation™ ENT and StealthStation FlexENT™ navigation systems with proprietary AxiEM™ EM tracking for precise confirmation1,2 of balloon seeker placement.

Simple system for balloon sinus surgery

Only three parts: balloon sinus seeker, tubing, and inflator.

Maxillary, sphenoid, and frontal designs

Four unique designs for the maxillary, sphenoid, and frontal (70° or 98°) sinus with three balloon diameter options.

Our balloons are designed after the commonly used ostium seekers. We added a balloon so you can locate and dilate the sinus ostia at the same time.

Safety features

Protective valve indicator to help prevent accidental over-inflation.3
