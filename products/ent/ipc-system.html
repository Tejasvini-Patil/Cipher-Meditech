ENT - Power Console

Integrated Power Console (IPC™) System

The multispecialty integrated power console (IPC™) powers tools used to remove soft tissue, hard tissue, and bone during ear, nose, and throat surgeries.

Category Power Console
Application ENT Surgery
Manufacturer Medtronic
Request Quote Download Brochure

Description

Powers Medtronic ENT handpieces

The IPC™ system powers Medtronic ear, nose, and throat (ENT) handpieces including the Straightshot™ M4 microdebrider, M5 microdebrider, and the Midas Rex™ MR8 drill.

From inferior turbinoplasties to complex skull base cases, the IPC™ platform can serve your full range of ENT surgery needs.

Features

Innovative irrigation control

During ENT surgery, the IPC™ suction-irrigation system provides a solution for irrigation control in the operating room. Its design includes optimized pre-set flow rates for all French tips. Plus, the IntelliFlow™ remote control allows for an easy on/off, tip size, and flow rate changes from the sterile field.

Multifunctional foot pedal for ergonomic control

The IPC™ foot pedal offers three buttons for maximum control during ENT surgery. Its non-slip design and compact size make it ideal for operating rooms. The hoop locks firmly in place, then collapses easily for storage.
