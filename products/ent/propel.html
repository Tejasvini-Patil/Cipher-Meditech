PROPEL® Sinus Implant

Bioabsorbable steroid-releasing implant designed to maintain sinus patency and reduce inflammation following sinus surgery.

Category Sinus Implant
Application Post-Surgical
Manufacturer Medtronic
Request Quote Download Brochure

Product Overview

PROPEL® is a spring-like, bioabsorbable implant that props open the ethmoid sinus and delivers an anti-inflammatory medication directly to the sinus lining. It is designed to decrease scarring and inflammation after sinus surgery.

The implant gradually dissolves over approximately 30 days while continuously releasing mometasone furoate, helping to improve surgical outcomes and reduce the need for post-operative interventions.

Key Features

  • Bioabsorbable design - no removal needed
  • Controlled steroid release over 30 days
  • Maintains sinus opening post-surgery
  • Reduces scarring and polyp recurrence
  • Clinically proven efficacy
  • Easy placement during FESS

Clinical Applications

  • Post-FESS sinus maintenance
  • Ethmoid sinus surgery
  • Chronic rhinosinusitis with polyps
  • Revision sinus surgery support

Technical Specifications

Material Bioabsorbable polymer
Drug Mometasone furoate
Duration ~30 days
Placement Intraoperative
Certification FDA, CE Mark
