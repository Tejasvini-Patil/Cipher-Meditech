Product Overview

The StealthStation™ ENT Navigation System represents the gold standard in image-guided surgery for ENT procedures. By fusing preoperative CT or MRI scans with real-time instrument tracking, surgeons can navigate complex sinus and skull base anatomy with unprecedented precision.

The system provides real-time visualization of instrument position relative to critical anatomical structures, reducing the risk of complications and enabling more complete and precise surgical interventions.

Key Features

  • Real-time 3D image-guided navigation
  • Electromagnetic and optical tracking options
  • CT/MRI image fusion capability
  • Automatic patient registration
  • Large HD touchscreen display
  • Intuitive user interface
  • Wide range of tracked instruments
  • Integration with powered instrumentation

Clinical Applications

  • Functional endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS)
  • Revision sinus surgery
  • Skull base tumor resection
  • CSF leak repair
  • Orbital decompression
  • Frontal sinus surgery
  • Pituitary surgery (transsphenoidal approach)
