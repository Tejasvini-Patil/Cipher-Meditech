Product Overview

The Midas Rex® MR8 System represents decades of innovation in high-speed surgical drilling technology. This pneumatic-powered system delivers exceptional speed, torque, and precision for the most demanding otologic and skull base procedures.

Known for its legendary reliability and precise control, the Midas Rex system enables surgeons to perform delicate bone work with confidence. The MR8 version features enhanced ergonomics and a comprehensive range of attachments and burrs.

Key Features

  • High-speed pneumatic motor (up to 80,000 RPM)
  • Exceptional torque consistency
  • Lightweight, ergonomic handpiece design
  • Wide range of attachment options
  • Comprehensive burr selection
  • Continuous irrigation capability
  • Proven long-term reliability
  • Quick-connect attachment system

Clinical Applications

  • Mastoidectomy
  • Cochlear implant surgery
  • Stapedectomy
  • Skull base tumor surgery
  • Acoustic neuroma resection
  • Lateral skull base approaches
  • Temporal bone surgery
  • Middle ear surgery
