Product Overview

The Diego® Elite Multidebrider System is a comprehensive powered surgical platform designed for a wide range of ENT procedures. With its versatile blade selection and precise motor control, it enables surgeons to perform tissue removal with accuracy and efficiency.

The system features an intuitive control interface, allowing surgeons to adjust speed, oscillation mode, and irrigation settings easily during procedures. Its robust design ensures reliable performance across thousands of procedures.

Key Features

  • Variable speed control (500-12,000 RPM)
  • Multiple oscillation modes
  • Integrated irrigation system
  • Wide range of blade options
  • Ergonomic handpiece design
  • Foot pedal control
  • Easy blade attachment system
  • Compatible with IPC console

Clinical Applications

  • Functional endoscopic sinus surgery
  • Adenoidectomy
  • Turbinate reduction
  • Polypectomy
  • Septoplasty assistance
  • Skull base surgery
  • Pediatric ENT procedures
