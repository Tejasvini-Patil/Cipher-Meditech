Product Overview

The Fusion™ ENT Navigation System brings the benefits of image-guided surgery to office-based settings and smaller operating environments. This portable electromagnetic navigation platform offers the precision of advanced navigation in a compact, easy-to-use format.

Ideal for balloon sinuplasty procedures and routine sinus surgery, the Fusion system provides real-time instrument tracking without the need for line-of-sight, making it particularly well-suited for endoscopic procedures.

Key Features

  • Compact, portable design
  • Electromagnetic tracking (no line-of-sight required)
  • Quick setup and registration
  • Intuitive tablet-based interface
  • Compatible with office-based CT scans
  • Wireless instrument tracking
  • Cost-effective navigation solution
  • Minimal training requirements

Clinical Applications

  • Balloon sinuplasty
  • Office-based sinus procedures
  • Functional endoscopic sinus surgery
  • Maxillary antrostomy
  • Ethmoidectomy
  • Sphenoidotomy
