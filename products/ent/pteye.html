Product Overview

The PT-Eye® Monitoring System provides real-time pressure feedback during surgical procedures, enabling surgeons to maintain safe operating parameters and optimize patient outcomes.

With intuitive visual displays and audible alerts, the system helps surgical teams monitor critical pressure levels throughout procedures, reducing the risk of complications.

Key Features

  • Real-time pressure monitoring
  • Visual and audible feedback
  • Intuitive display interface
  • Customizable alert thresholds
  • Easy integration with surgical workflow
  • Enhanced patient safety

Clinical Applications

  • ENT surgical procedures
  • Sinus surgery monitoring
  • Pressure-sensitive procedures
  • Intraoperative safety monitoring
