Cochlear Implant System

Advanced cochlear implant system for patients with severe to profound sensorineural hearing loss, providing direct electrical stimulation of the auditory nerve for improved sound perception.

Category Hearing Implants
Application Otology
Type Implantable Device
Request Quote Download Brochure

Product Overview

The Cochlear Implant System represents breakthrough technology in hearing restoration for patients who receive limited benefit from conventional hearing aids. The system bypasses damaged portions of the ear and directly stimulates the auditory nerve, providing a sense of sound to the user.

The system consists of an external sound processor worn behind the ear and an internal implant surgically placed under the skin. Advanced signal processing algorithms and electrode array design work together to deliver clear, natural sound quality for speech understanding and music appreciation.

Key Features

  • Advanced sound processing with multiple programs
  • MRI-compatible internal implant design
  • Slim, ergonomic external processor
  • Wireless connectivity to smartphones and accessories
  • Rechargeable and disposable battery options
  • Water-resistant external components
  • Atraumatic electrode array for hearing preservation
  • Remote programming capability

Clinical Indications

  • Severe to profound bilateral sensorineural hearing loss
  • Limited benefit from appropriately fitted hearing aids
  • Post-lingual and pre-lingual deafness
  • Adults and pediatric patients (12 months and older)
  • Single-sided deafness with normal contralateral hearing
