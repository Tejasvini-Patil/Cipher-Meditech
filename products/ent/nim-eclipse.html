Product Overview

The NIM-Eclipse® System is Medtronic's flagship multi-modality intraoperative neurophysiological monitoring (IONM) platform. Designed for complex surgical procedures, it combines EMG, SSEPs, MEPs, and other monitoring modalities in a single comprehensive system.

With its advanced software and high-channel capacity, the NIM-Eclipse enables monitoring specialists to simultaneously track multiple neural pathways, providing surgeons with real-time feedback on neurological function throughout lengthy and complex procedures.

Key Features

  • Multi-modality monitoring (EMG, SSEPs, MEPs, EEG, ABR)
  • Up to 32 channels of acquisition
  • Advanced signal processing algorithms
  • Customizable monitoring protocols
  • Large high-resolution display
  • Network connectivity for remote viewing
  • Comprehensive reporting and documentation
  • Integrated stimulator options

Clinical Applications

  • Complex skull base surgery
  • Acoustic neuroma resection
  • Spinal cord tumor surgery
  • Aortic surgery
  • Carotid endarterectomy
  • Brainstem procedures
  • Complex ENT reconstructive surgery
