Product Overview

The Straightshot® M5 Microdebrider represents the latest generation in powered sinus surgery instruments. Designed with a straight blade orientation and advanced motor technology, it delivers exceptional cutting performance for functional endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS) procedures.

The M5 handpiece features an optimized blade window design that enhances tissue capture and reduces clogging, while the ergonomic grip design reduces surgeon fatigue during extended procedures.

Key Features

  • High-speed motor up to 15,000 RPM
  • Straight blade configuration for optimal visibility
  • Enhanced blade window for improved tissue capture
  • Continuous irrigation port
  • Lightweight, ergonomic design
  • Compatible with full blade portfolio
  • Autoclavable handpiece
  • Reduced noise operation

Clinical Applications

  • Functional endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS)
  • Chronic rhinosinusitis treatment
  • Nasal polyp removal
  • Turbinate reduction
  • Ethmoidectomy
  • Sphenoidotomy
  • Frontal sinusotomy
