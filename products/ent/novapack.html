Product Overview

Novapack® Nasal Packing provides effective hemostasis and support for wound healing following nasal and sinus surgery. Designed for patient comfort and easy removal, it helps manage post-operative bleeding.

The advanced material design ensures effective compression while minimizing patient discomfort during the healing period. Available in various sizes to accommodate different clinical needs.

Key Features

  • Effective hemostasis control
  • Patient-comfortable design
  • Easy insertion and removal
  • Multiple sizes available
  • Supports wound healing
  • Biocompatible materials

Clinical Applications

  • Post-sinus surgery packing
  • Septoplasty support
  • Turbinate surgery
  • Nasal trauma management
  • Epistaxis control
