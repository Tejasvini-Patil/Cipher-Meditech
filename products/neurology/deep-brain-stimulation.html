Product Overview

The Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) System delivers precisely controlled electrical pulses to specific brain regions, helping to regulate abnormal brain activity associated with movement disorders. This FDA-approved therapy offers significant symptom improvement for patients with Parkinson's disease, essential tremor, and dystonia.

Key Features

  • Rechargeable or primary cell neurostimulator options
  • Directional lead technology for precise targeting
  • MRI conditional components
  • Patient programmer for therapy adjustments
  • Clinician programming software
  • Long-term reliability and durability

Clinical Indications

  • Parkinson's disease
  • Essential tremor
  • Dystonia
  • Obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD)
