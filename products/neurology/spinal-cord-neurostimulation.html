Product Overview

Spinal Cord Stimulation delivers mild electrical pulses to the spinal cord, interrupting pain signals before they reach the brain. This reversible therapy provides significant pain relief for patients with chronic pain conditions who haven't responded to other treatments.

Key Features

  • Multiple stimulation waveforms
  • Rechargeable or primary cell IPG
  • Full-body MRI conditional
  • Patient remote control
  • Adaptive stimulation technology
  • Customizable therapy programs

Clinical Indications

  • Failed back surgery syndrome
  • Complex regional pain syndrome
  • Chronic leg and back pain
  • Diabetic neuropathy
  • Angina pectoris
