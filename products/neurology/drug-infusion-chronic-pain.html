Drug Infusion System for Chronic Pain

Programmable implantable pump system for targeted intrathecal drug delivery in patients with chronic, intractable pain.

CategoryDrug Delivery
ApplicationPain Management
Request Quote Download Brochure

Product Overview

The Drug Infusion System delivers precise amounts of medication directly to the intrathecal space around the spinal cord. This targeted approach requires significantly lower doses than oral medications, reducing side effects while providing superior pain relief.

Key Features

  • Programmable flow rates
  • Multiple dosing modes
  • Refillable drug reservoir
  • Patient therapy controller
  • MRI conditional design
  • Long battery life

Clinical Applications

  • Chronic non-malignant pain
  • Cancer-related pain
  • Failed back surgery syndrome
  • Complex regional pain syndrome
