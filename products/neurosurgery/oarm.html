O-arm™ surgical imaging system

The O-arm™ surgical imaging system offers on-demand imaging, robotic positioning, and device mobility for spinal and cranial procedures.

CategorySurgical Imaging
ManufacturerMedtronic
Request Quote Download Brochure

Description

Procedure-specific workflows for the information you need

The O-arm™ surgical imaging system is an interoperative 2-D/3-D imaging device that can help confirm alignment and accuracy during spinal procedures.

Because you can determine positioning before the operation ends, the O-arm™ system may help improve the chances of a successful procedure without the need for reoperation.

Features

Superior image quality

With the ability to perform both 2-D and 3-D scans, the O-arm™ generates exceptional and versatile images that assist in surgical planning, validate accurate placement during procedures, and facilitate post-operative positioning evaluation.

Gain confidence across the entire spinal procedural range with the superior volume and power of the O-arm™ system:

  • Obtain highly detailed images with a full 360-degree rotation around the patient.
  • Image a patient’s entire pelvis with a wide 40 cm field of view.
  • Accommodate challenging procedures involving heavier patients or individuals with unique anatomical characteristics using the powerful generator within the O-arm™ system.

Get the image clarity you deserve without any compromises to your surgical workflow.
