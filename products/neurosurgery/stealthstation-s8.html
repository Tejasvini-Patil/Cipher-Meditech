Neuro Surgery - Navigation

StealthStation™ S8 navigation platform

StealthStation™ S8 is a surgical navigation system that precisely locates anatomical structures in open or percutaneous neurosurgical and spine procedures.

CategoryNeuro Navigation
ManufacturerMedtronic
Request Quote Download Brochure

Description

Track the location of surgical instruments throughout a procedure

The StealthStation™ S8 navigation system introduces the most advanced version of stealth technology to help guide you during spinal and cranial procedures. The system combines hardware, software, tracking algorithms, image data merging, and specialized instruments. It enables clear visualization and surgical navigation of the cranium and every segment in the spine.

Features

The visualization you need for your most challenging procedures

The StealthStation™ surgical navigation system offers both optical and electromagnetic (EM) tracking capabilities, integration with external devices like microscopes. It also offers a broad array of instruments and core software applications for neurosurgery and spine procedures, including:

  • Biopsy
  • Tumor Resection
  • Catheter placement
  • Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) lead placement
