Product Overview

Corpectomy devices are expandable cages used to replace removed vertebral bodies. These implants provide immediate structural support while promoting bone fusion across multiple spinal segments.

Key Features

  • In-situ expansion capability
  • Anatomical endplate options
  • Bone graft containment
  • Multiple height options
  • Titanium or PEEK construction

Clinical Indications

  • Tumor resection reconstruction
  • Trauma reconstruction
  • Degenerative conditions
  • Infection debridement

Technical Specifications

TypeExpandable
