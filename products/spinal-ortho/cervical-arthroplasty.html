Product Overview

Cervical disc arthroplasty provides an alternative to fusion for treating symptomatic cervical disc disease. These devices maintain natural motion at the treated level, potentially reducing stress on adjacent segments.

Key Features

  • Preserves natural cervical motion
  • Reduces adjacent segment stress
  • Multiple size options
  • Proven long-term outcomes
  • MRI compatible materials

Clinical Indications

  • Symptomatic cervical disc disease
  • Radiculopathy
  • Myelopathy
  • Single or multi-level disease
