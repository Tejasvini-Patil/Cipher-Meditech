Balloon Kyphoplasty

Minimally invasive vertebral augmentation procedure using inflatable bone tamps to restore vertebral body height and reduce pain from compression fractures.

CategoryVertebral Augmentation

Product Overview

Balloon Kyphoplasty is a minimally invasive procedure that uses inflatable bone tamps to create a cavity within the fractured vertebra. This allows for controlled cement delivery and potential height restoration, providing pain relief and improved function.

Key Features

  • Inflatable bone tamps for cavity creation
  • Controlled cement delivery system
  • Height restoration capability
  • Low-viscosity bone cement
  • Complete procedural kits

Clinical Indications

  • Vertebral compression fractures
  • Osteoporotic fractures
  • Pathologic fractures
  • Traumatic fractures
