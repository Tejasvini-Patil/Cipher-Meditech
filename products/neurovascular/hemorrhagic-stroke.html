Product Overview

Our hemorrhagic stroke product portfolio includes advanced endovascular solutions for the treatment of intracranial aneurysms and vascular malformations. These minimally invasive devices enable effective treatment with reduced patient morbidity.

Product Categories

  • Detachable Coils - Bare platinum and coated coils for aneurysm occlusion
  • Flow Diverters - Stent-like devices that redirect blood flow away from aneurysms
  • Intrasaccular Devices - Woven mesh devices for wide-neck aneurysms
  • Liquid Embolics - Injectable agents for AVM treatment
  • Stents - Intracranial stents for stent-assisted coiling
  • Access Catheters - Guide catheters and microcatheters

Clinical Applications

  • Intracranial aneurysms (ruptured and unruptured)
  • Arteriovenous malformations (AVMs)
  • Dural arteriovenous fistulas
  • Carotid cavernous fistulas
