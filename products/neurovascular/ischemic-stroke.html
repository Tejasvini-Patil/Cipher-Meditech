Product Overview

Our ischemic stroke portfolio provides cutting-edge solutions for mechanical thrombectomy in acute ischemic stroke patients. These devices enable rapid clot removal and vessel recanalization, significantly improving patient outcomes when time is critical.

Product Categories

  • Stent Retrievers - Self-expanding devices for clot capture and removal
  • Aspiration Catheters - Large bore catheters for direct aspiration thrombectomy
  • Combined Approach Devices - Systems supporting both stent retriever and aspiration techniques
  • Distal Access Catheters - Navigation support for intracranial access
  • Guide Catheters - Proximal support and aspiration capability
  • Guidewires - Specialty neurovascular guidewires

Clinical Applications

  • Acute ischemic stroke with large vessel occlusion
  • Internal carotid artery occlusion
  • Middle cerebral artery occlusion
  • Basilar artery occlusion
